Our Story

CoinOfVantage was founded with a single belief: that digital asset markets, despite their volatility, reward disciplined capital allocation and rigorous risk management above all else.

We chose Liechtenstein as our regulatory home for its forward-thinking approach to digital assets and its position within the European Economic Area — allowing us to serve qualified investors across the Benelux region under a robust, passported framework.

Our fund is built for the long term. We are not chasing hype cycles. We are building a sustainable institution.
